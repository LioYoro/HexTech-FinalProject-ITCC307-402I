‚ùå "Cannot POST /api/predict"

   ‚Üí Run: python train_model.py first
   ‚Üí Check: models/ folder has .pkl files

   ‚Üí Copy ENERGY_DATA_CLEANED.csv to frontend/data/
   ‚Üí And to backend/data/
